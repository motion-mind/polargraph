✓ Flash complete — next steps

  1. The board restarts and creates a WiFi network called Polargraph-Setup
  2. Connect your phone or laptop to that network (password: polargraph)
  3. Open http://192.168.4.1/wifi in your browser
  4. Enter your home WiFi name and password, click Save
  5. The board reboots and joins your network
  6. Find its IP address on your router's device list
  7. Open http://<ip>/ — you're ready to draw!
All future firmware updates use http://<ip>/update — no USB needed again.
Troubleshooting
"Failed to connect" Hold BOOT button while clicking Flash
Port not listed Install CP210x or CH340 driver
Port in use Close serial monitors / Arduino IDE
Linux: permission denied sudo usermod -aG dialout $USER then log out
